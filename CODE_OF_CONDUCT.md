# Code Of Conduct

DEALIoT uses a pragmatic professional conduct policy.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise, respectful, and evidence-driven.
- Critique technical decisions, not people.
- Surface risk early when a change affects security, operations, or compliance.
- Respect confidentiality for adopters, pilots, datasets, incidents, and partner discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or adopter information without approval.
- Misrepresenting production readiness, compliance status, or adoption references.
- Intentionally bypassing security controls or review requirements.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy. Repeated or severe violations can lead to blocked participation in issues, pull requests, or community channels.

Report conduct concerns through the security or support channels in `SUPPORT.md`. Do not disclose sensitive details in public issues.
